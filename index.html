Athena Jobs Q&A

Reference these answers when teammates and candidates ask how the portal works. They mirror the guidance we share with members every day.

Quality & Coverage

How does Athena source the roles that appear on the portal?

Roles are submitted directly by founders, hiring managers, and operators across the Athena network. The concierge team reviews every update before it goes live, so each listing reflects a real and active search.

How often are listings refreshed or retired?

Hiring signals are checked throughout the week with the teams running each search. When a role is filled or paused we remove it, and we welcome flags from members if something looks out of date so we can act quickly.

Can I focus on remote or location-specific opportunities?

Yes. Listings are tagged by location, function, and whether they are remote, hybrid, or on-site. Use the filters in the portal to zero in on the geographies and work styles that matter to you.

Access & Eligibility

Who can access jobs.athena.live?

Access is reserved for members of the Athena community—operators, founders, and partners who collaborate with us. Each login is tied to a specific person so we can keep the environment trusted and high-signal.

How do I request access for a teammate or chief of staff?

Share the name and email of the person who needs access with your Athena point of contact. We provision new seats quickly and confirm once their account is live so you can collaborate without sharing credentials.

What happens after I'm invited to the portal?

Invited users receive a secure link to set their password and confirm their profile. From there they can browse live searches, follow the teams they care about, and start requesting intros directly from the listings.

Working with Hiring Teams

How do I keep tabs on new or high-signal roles?

The home feed surfaces the most recent updates first and highlights searches with strong hiring signal. Follow the teams you care about inside the portal and check in regularly—we update several times each week as founders share new needs.

Can I connect with the hiring team from a listing?

Yes. Each role includes context on who is driving the search plus a direct way to request an intro or contact them. Bring your background and interest when you reach out so the conversation moves quickly.

What if I need more context or spot something inaccurate?

Send a quick note to your Athena contact with the role link. We'll confirm the details with the hiring team and update or remove the listing if the search has wrapped.

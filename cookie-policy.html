What are web beacons?

Athena Live occasionally advertises on third-party websites. As part of our efforts to track campaign success, we may use visitor identification technologies such as web beacons (also called action tags). These technologies compile aggregated statistics about visitors who arrive at the website after seeing our ads. We do not use web beacons to access personal information.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored in your browser that allows the website to remember information about your session. Cookies are sent back to the website on each subsequent visit, or to another webpage that recognizes that cookie. Cookies help the website function, improve efficiency, and provide information to site owners.

Cookies help you navigate between pages efficiently, remember preferences, and improve user experience. They can also help ensure that adverts you see online are more relevant to your interests.

What types of cookies do we use?

The website uses cookies that perform four functions:

  • Essential/strictly necessary cookies, which are required for the website to function.
  • Performance cookies, which help us measure the website's performance and improve your experience.
  • Functional cookies, which remember your preferences and settings.
  • Marketing/targeting cookies, which are set by third parties and help us measure campaign effectiveness and deliver relevant content.

We may use first-party cookies served directly by Athena Live, as well as third-party cookies served on our behalf by trusted partners. The specific cookies used may vary as we update our services.

How to control or delete cookies

You have the right to choose whether or not to accept cookies. If you refuse optional cookies, you may still use the website, but some features may be limited.

You can change your cookie settings at any time by clicking the Customize cookies button at the top of this policy.

As an alternative, you can block all cookies by activating the setting on your browser that allows you to refuse the setting of all or some cookies. If you block all cookies (including essential cookies), you may not be able to access all or parts of our site.

Cookies that have been set in the past

If you have disabled one or more cookies, we may still use information collected from cookies prior to your disabled preference being set; however, we will stop using the disabled cookie to collect any further information.

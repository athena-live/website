Editorial Independence Policy

Last updated: 2026

Purpose

Athena Live News maintains strict editorial independence to protect the integrity of our reporting. News coverage is produced without interference from advertisers, sponsors, investors, or product teams.

Editorial Authority

  • Editors and reporters control story selection, framing, and publication timing.
  • Business, marketing, and product teams do not approve editorial content.
  • Coverage is based on public interest, verified facts, and relevance.

Separation From Commercial Interests

  • Paid content is labeled clearly and separated from news coverage.
  • We do not sell favorable coverage or suppress unfavorable reporting.
  • Editorial staff do not accept gifts, payments, or favors that could influence coverage.

Conflicts of Interest

  • Reporters disclose potential conflicts to their editor.
  • Assignments are reassigned when a conflict could compromise impartiality.
  • Material relationships are disclosed to readers when relevant.

Transparency

When Athena Live News covers Athena Live LLC or affiliated products, we disclose the relationship within the story to provide full context.

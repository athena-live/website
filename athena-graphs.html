Graph Planner

Visualize plans as routes. Save iterations. Collaborate through a shared backend.

Turn complex projects into navigable graphs—where every decision has a path, every path has a history, and every teammate stays in sync.

Open Graph Planner View Demo

What it is

Plans aren’t documents. They’re living systems. Graph Planner is a planning workspace that models your strategy like a route map: nodes, edges, constraints, alternatives, and checkpoints—built for iteration and shared execution.

Athena Graphs also provides AI-powered flowchart and diagram generation, producing graph and network models with AI agents.

Key benefits

Route-based planning

Map goals to steps and dependencies as a clear route. See what blocks what, and what unlocks momentum.

Versioned iterations

Save branches, compare paths, and roll back instantly. Iterate without losing history.

Shared backend collaboration

One source of truth for the whole team. Comments, assignments, and updates stay attached to the graph.

How it works

  1. Draft a route — goals, tasks, dependencies, constraints.
  2. Branch & iterate — test alternatives without rewriting everything.
  3. Ship with alignment — share the graph, track changes, execute together.

Feature highlights

  • Nodes & dependencies: model reality, not wishful thinking.
  • Branches: explore Plan A / B / C cleanly.
  • Snapshots: save milestones, decisions, and rationale.
  • Collaboration layer: roles, comments, and shared state.
  • Backend-first: structured data you can integrate later.
  • History built-in: track every change across the graph.

Use cases

  • Product roadmaps with real dependency awareness.
  • Launch planning across marketing, engineering, and ops.
  • Complex client delivery plans with versioned changes.
  • Strategy exploration: “If we cut this, what breaks?”

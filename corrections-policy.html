Corrections Policy

Last updated: 2026

Commitment to Accuracy

Athena Live News corrects errors promptly and transparently. We take all reports of inaccuracies seriously and investigate them as quickly as possible.

How to Request a Correction

Email info@athena.live with a link to the story, the specific issue, and supporting evidence.

How We Handle Corrections

  • We review the report, source materials, and editorial notes.
  • If an error is confirmed, we correct the story and add a correction note.
  • Material updates include a timestamp and a brief explanation of the change.
  • If a claim cannot be verified, we remove or amend it.

Clarifications

If a story is factually correct but could be misunderstood, we may add a clarification to provide context.

Reader Transparency

Correction and clarification notes are placed near the relevant content or at the end of the story.

Contact

Corrections and questions: info@athena.live

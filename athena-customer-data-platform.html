Athena Customer Data Platform

Unify, enrich, and activate customer data across every touchpoint. Athena CDP is a centralized intelligence layer that turns behavioral signals into real-time segments, actionable insights, and personalized experiences.

What the Platform Does

Athena CDP connects data streams from product, marketing, and operations to build a full-funnel customer narrative. Every identity, event, and attribute is normalized so teams can act with confidence.

Unified Profiles

Merge behavioral, transactional, and engagement data into a single customer view.

Real-Time Segmentation

Build dynamic cohorts based on live signals, campaign history, and intent scoring.

Activation Pipelines

Deliver audiences, alerts, and playbooks to every downstream tool in minutes.

Core Capabilities

  • Identity resolution across devices and channels
  • Event streaming and schema governance
  • Customer health scoring and lifecycle analysis
  • Audience orchestration with rule-based automation
  • Enrichment with first- and third-party sources
  • Data quality monitoring and alerts
  • Revenue attribution and funnel insights
  • Consent and privacy-aware governance
  • Secure workspace collaboration

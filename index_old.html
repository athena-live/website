Athena Jobs Q&A

Reference these answers when teammates and candidates ask how the portal works. They mirror the guidance we share with operators, candidates, and hiring teams every day.

Quality & Coverage

How does Athena source the roles that appear on the portal?

Roles are submitted directly by founders, hiring managers, and operators across the Athena network. The concierge team reviews every update before it goes live, so each listing reflects a real and active search.

How often are listings refreshed or retired?

Hiring signals are checked throughout the week with the teams running each search. When a role is filled or paused we remove it, and we welcome flags from the community if something looks out of date so we can act quickly.

Can I focus on remote or location-specific opportunities?

Yes. Listings are tagged by location, function, and whether they are remote, hybrid, or on-site. Use the filters in the portal to zero in on the geographies and work styles that matter to you.

Access & Eligibility

Who can access jobs.athena.live?

Everyone is welcome. Create a free account to explore the portal instantly—no invite code, referrals, or waitlist. We designed the experience so you can get to the opportunities as fast as possible.

How do I onboard a teammate or chief of staff?

Share the jobs.athena.live link with them—they can spin up their own login in minutes. We encourage each teammate to keep their own profile so they can follow companies and manage alerts separately.

What happens after I create an account?

You choose your interests, follow teams, and start tracking roles right away. Personalize alerts, save searches, and request intros directly from listings—all without waiting on manual approvals.

Working with Hiring Teams

How do I keep tabs on new or high-signal roles?

The home feed surfaces the most recent updates first and highlights searches with strong hiring signal. Follow the teams you care about inside the portal and check in regularly—we update several times each week as founders share new needs.

Can I connect with the hiring team from a listing?

Yes. Each role includes context on who is driving the search plus a direct way to request an intro or contact them. Bring your background and interest when you reach out so the conversation moves quickly.

What if I need more context or spot something inaccurate?

Flag it right from the listing or drop us a note at info@athena.live. We'll verify the details with the hiring team and refresh or retire the post so the feed stays high-signal.
